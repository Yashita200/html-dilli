Morning:
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Breakfast
Start your day with a hearty breakfast at a local cafe or restaurant near your accommodation.

9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Red Fort
Explore the historic Red Fort. Take your time to admire the stunning architecture and learn about its significance in Indian history.

Late Morning:
10:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Chandni Chowk
Head to Chandni Chowk, a bustling market. Stroll through the narrow lanes, explore the shops, and experience the vibrant atmosphere.

Lunch:
11:30 AM - 1:00 PM: Lunch
Enjoy a traditional lunch at one of the many eateries in Chandni Chowk. Try local street food or dine in a historical restaurant.

Afternoon:
1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: India Gate
Visit India Gate, a war memorial. Take a leisurely walk around the area, enjoy the greenery, and pay your respects to the Amar Jawan Jyoti.

2:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Jama Masjid
Explore Jama Masjid, one of the largest mosques in India. Take in the architectural beauty and tranquility of this historical site.

Late Afternoon/Evening:
4:00 PM - 5:30 PM: Shopping and Leisure
Spend some time shopping or relaxing at a nearby cafe. Consider exploring the markets around Connaught Place or Janpath.

Dinner:
5:30 PM - 7:00 PM: Dinner
Have dinner at a restaurant of your choice. You might want to try a place that offers a mix of Mughlai and Delhi street food.

Night:
7:00 PM - Onward: Optional Evening Activity
