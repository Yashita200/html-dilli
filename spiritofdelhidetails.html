Morning:
8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Breakfast
Start your day with breakfast at a local cafe near your accommodation.

9:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Lotus Temple
Begin your day with a visit to the Lotus Temple. Explore the serene surroundings and the unique lotus-shaped architecture.

Late Morning:
10:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Travel to Hauz Khas
Travel to Hauz Khas, either by cab or public transportation.

11:30 AM - 1:00 PM: Hauz Khas Village
Explore Hauz Khas Village, a trendy neighborhood with art galleries, boutiques, and cafes. Don't miss the historic Hauz Khas complex with its ancient reservoir.

Lunch:
1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Lunch
Enjoy lunch at a restaurant or cafe in Hauz Khas Village.

Afternoon:
2:30 PM - 3:30 PM: Travel to Dilli Haat
Travel to Dilli Haat, either by cab or public transportation.

3:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Dilli Haat
Explore Dilli Haat, a cultural and crafts bazaar. Browse through handicraft stalls, enjoy cultural performances, and try diverse regional cuisines.

Late Afternoon/Evening:
5:00 PM - 6:30 PM: Travel to Sarojini Nagar
Travel to Sarojini Nagar, known for its bustling market.

6:30 PM - 7:30 PM: Sarojini Nagar Market
Explore the Sarojini Nagar Market for shopping. It's famous for trendy clothes, accessories, and footwear.

Dinner:
7:30 PM - 9:00 PM: Dinner
Have dinner at a local restaurant or try street food in Sarojini Nagar.
